About Us

Welcome to our Biokinetics Practice, founded by two dedicated professionals passionate about improving health through movement. Our mission is driven by hard work and dedication to offer personalized, evidence-based programs tailored to your needs. Whether you're recovering from an injury, managing a chronic condition, or enhancing your fitness, we're here to support you with our passionate approach to biokinetics. Join us on the path to optimal health and well-being.

Vision

Empowerment: We empower individuals to take control of their health and well-being through personalized movement programs.

Mission

To revolutionize the approach to health and fitness by providing comprehensive biokinetic services that inspire, educate, and empower individuals to achieve their full potential.

Meet Our Team

Ruth Ramabu

Biokineticist

Ruth Ramabu completed her Diploma and B-tech degree at Tswhane University of technology, she then furthered her studies and completed a health science honours degree in Biokineticis at WITS. She is registered with the Health professional council (HPCSA) and Biokineticist Assocoation of South Africa (BASA). Ruth has a long history of sport and conditioning working for TUT High Perfromance Basketball and Netball, as well as working for the Midrand Back and Neck Centre and Physio and Wellness centre.
